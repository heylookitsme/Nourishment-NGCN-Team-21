About

TEAM 21

This is us! Members of this team are from the U.S., Iraq-Kurdistan region, and Palestine.
They started working on this website/project during the week of February 16th. They brainstormed ideas ranging from global
awareness for violence against women to advocating for the distribution of factual information about COVID-19,
before deciding on the topic of water and food insecurity. They researched the topic and found resources that would aid in either
creating the website or ensuring every member knew what work had to be completed. Within 10 weeks, this team created the website
you're viewing now. This team thanks you for engaging with this website, and hopes it will do some good to those that need help or education
on water and food insecurity.


About World Learning and NGCN

World Learning's mission is "to work globally to enhance the capacity and commitment of individuals, institutions, and communities to create a more sustainable, peaceful, and just world".
NextGen Coders Network "brings together multidisciplinary university students and young professionals from Iraq, the Palestinian Territories, and the U.S. for a virtual exchange to learn coding and collaborate on a website or application that tackles a grand challenge".
